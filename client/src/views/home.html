Cup of Sugar
Cup of Sugar is a Twin Cities based peer-to-peer rental platform where users can both lend and borrow various items and products. While allowing product owners to earn a return on rentable items, renters can easily borrow products at affordable rates without having to pay retail prices. Lend and borrow unused items locally, or earn easy money from products laying around your home and garage.
